# FCAJ Community Day - May 2026

**Event Name:** FCAJ Community Day  
**Date & Time:** 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM, Saturday, May 23, 2026  
**Location:** Bitexco Financial Tower, 2 Hai Trieu Street, Saigon Ward, Ho Chi Minh City  
**Role:** Attendee  

### Event Content
The FCAJ Community Day featured several insightful sessions on AI, cloud optimization, and software development delivered by industry experts:
- **Context Is Everything: Making AI Actually Work for You (Speaker: Tinh Truong):** Covered why AI fails without context, introduced the concept of a Second AI Brain, and shared practical tips for prompt engineering.
- **Friendly AI Assistant with Amazon Quick (Speaker: Anh Pham):** Explored intelligent workflows, data exploration, and report building using natural language without writing any code.
- **From Edge To Origin: CloudFront as Your Foundation (Speaker: Thinh Nguyen):** Discussed cost optimization, security, and performance enhancement using Amazon CloudFront.
- **36 hrs with LotusHacks – Building UTMorpho from Idea to Reality (Speaker: Team VIB):** Shared a real hackathon journey, from initial brainstorming to building a product under a strict 36-hour deadline.
- **Non-Determinism of "Deterministic" LLM Settings (Speaker: Duc Dao):** Deep dive into how LLMs choose tokens and the reality behind the assumption that temperature=0 guarantees determinism.
- **Enterprise-Grade Multi-Agent System (Speaker: Vy Lam):** Explored a multi-agent paradigm for startup credit scoring, balancing regulatory compliance with operational ROI.

### Outcomes and Values Gained
Going to this event really changed my perspective on how AI is actually used in real companies, rather than just doing tutorials.

**What stuck with me the most:**
1. **Prompt Engineering & Contextual AI:** I realized that giving AI the right context is way more important than using fancy prompt templates. This gave me a lot of ideas for my own internal tools.
2. **Architecture Optimization:** Thinh's session on CloudFront was super practical. I learned a few neat tricks to lower latency and secure APIs, which I'll definitely use for my Serverless projects.
3. **Multi-Agent Systems:** I always thought of AI as a single chatbot. Seeing how multiple agents can talk to each other to handle complex logic like credit scoring was mind-blowing.
4. **Hackathon Mindset:** Hearing Team VIB's story about building a product in 36 hours really motivated me to stop over-engineering and just focus on shipping the core features first.
